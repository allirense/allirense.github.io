Why I'm Doing This

I've been working in web development and design for nearly two decades, but in 2013, I started to get serious about writing fiction.

I got involved in the writing community, both online and at home in West Michigan. I've met so many wonderful, creative, and supportive people. I've seen many writers and authors struggle with pulling together a web presence. Sure, there's social media, but many authors want a place on the web that's more their own.

Being in the industry, I've stayed on top of the latest trends and I understand the trade offs.

Helping authors and writers navigate the many available website options is one way I can give back to the writing community I've grown to love.
